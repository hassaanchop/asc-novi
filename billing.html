Billing Information

Understanding Your Statement

You Will Receive Four Separate Bills

After your procedure, you will receive separate bills from each provider involved in your care. This is normal and expected:

  • › Your surgeon
  • › Your nurse anesthetist
  • › The facility (ASC Novi)
  • › Your anesthesiologist

You are responsible for any co-pays and deductibles per your insurance plan.

Before Your Procedure

To minimize surprises, we encourage you to:

  • › Contact your insurance company to verify benefits and get pre-authorization
  • › Ask about your deductible, out-of-pocket maximum, and co-insurance
  • › Confirm that ASC Novi and your surgeon are in-network
  • › Call our billing office with any coverage questions at (586) 722-4702
