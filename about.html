Our Facility

Built for Comfort. Designed for Excellence.

Bone and Joint Surgery Center of Novi is a modern, fully accredited ambulatory surgery center located in Novi, Michigan. Our facility was purpose-built to provide the highest quality surgical care in an environment that feels more like a boutique health center than a hospital.

We feature state-of-the-art operating suites equipped with the latest technology, spacious and comfortable pre-operative and recovery areas, and private patient bays that respect your dignity and privacy.

Our central location at 26750 Providence Parkway, Suite 100 offers easy access for patients throughout metro Detroit — with ample parking, single-level entry, and a welcoming front desk team ready to assist you.
